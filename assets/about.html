ABOUT US

Our History

In 1999, amidst Nigeria's educational reform, Bosom Private Schools was established in Lagos. Founded with a vision to provide quality education, it quickly gained recognition for its innovative teaching methods and commitment to holistic development. Over the years, Bosom Private School has evolved into a renowned institution, producing notable alumni who excel in various fields both nationally and internationally. Its rich history is marked by academic excellence, cultural diversity, and a strong sense of community, making it a beacon of education in Nigeria.
